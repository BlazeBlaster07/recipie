Recipie of Mango Shake



Ingredients

  • 2 ripe mangoes
  • 1 cup of milk (chilled)
  • 2-3 tablespoons of sugar (optional, depending on sweetness of mangoes)
  • A handful of ice cubes
  • A scoop of vanilla ice cream (optional, for extra creaminess)

Steps

  1. Peel and chop the mangoes: Remove the skin and cut the mango flesh into small pieces.
  2. Blend the mangoes: Add the chopped mangoes to a blender
  3. Add milk: Pour in the chilled milk.
  4. Add sugar: If your mangoes aren't very sweet, add the sugar.
  5. Blend: Blend until smooth
  6. Add ice: Toss in the ice cubes and blend again
  7. Optional: Add a scoop of vanilla ice cream and blend for a creamier shake.
