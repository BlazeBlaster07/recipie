Recipie of Chai



Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 teaspoons of tea leaves (or 1 tea bag)
  • 2 teaspoons of sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 2 cardamom pods, crushed (optional)
  • A small piece of ginger, crushed (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil water: Pour 1 cup of water into a saucepan and bring it to a boil.
  2. Add tea leaves: Add 2 teaspoons of tea leaves (or 1 tea bag) to the boiling water.
  3. Add spices: Optional but adds a lovely flavor â€“ crush and add 2 cardamom pods and a small piece of ginger.
  4. Simmer: Let it simmer for about 2-3 minutes.
  5. Add milk: Pour in 1 cup of milk.
  6. Add sugar: Add 2 teaspoons of sugar (or adjust to taste).
  7. Boil: Bring the mixture to a boil again, then let it simmer for another 2-3 minutes.
  8. Strain and serve: Strain the tea into a cup and enjoy your chai!
